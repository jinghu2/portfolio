Fluff Stuff

I have designed an online shopping website for the Fluff Stuff pillow company. The persona is young and trendy people who want to buy beautifully designed pillows. To fit that goal, this website is visually pleasing and fast-paced, designed to target our young users.

Ann Arbor

The overall design goal of my website is to record my memories there as a beautiful journal. The viewers are most likely my family and friends who are interested in knowing more about my life. Unlike TripAdvisor that introduces places meaningful to the tourists, my website records places that are meaningful and personal to me.
